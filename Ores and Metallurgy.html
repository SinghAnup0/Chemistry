Q1: Which of the following is NOT a non-ferrous metallic mineral?

Source: RRB NTPC 04.03.2021 (Shift-II) Stage Ist
Correct! On the basis of composition, minerals are classified mainly as metallic and non-metallic minerals. • Metallic minerals contain metal in raw form. Metals are hard substances that conduct heat and electricity and have a characteristic lustre or shine. Iron ore, bauxite, manganese ore are some examples. • Metallic minerals may be ferrous or non-ferrous. • Ferrous minerals like iron ore, contain iron. • A non-ferrous mineral does not contain iron but may contain some other metal such as gold, silver, copper or lead. • Non-metallic minerals do not contain metals. Limestone, mica and gypsum are examples of such minerals. The mineral fuels like coal and petroleum are also non-metallic minerals.
